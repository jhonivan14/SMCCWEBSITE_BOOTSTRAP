About SMCC

SMCC VISION

Saint Michael College of Caraga envisions to be a University by 2035 and upholds spiritual formation and excellence in teaching, service, and research.

SMCC MISSION

SMCC shall provide spiritual formation and learning culture that will ensure students with excellent and rewarding learning experiences that transform lives, abound spirituality, develop skills, and prepare future leaders.

SMCC shall engage in dynamic, innovative, and interdisciplinary research to advance and achieve institutional initiatives.

SMCC shall commit to serve diverse and local communities by fostering innovations through service-learning that enhances reciprocal community partnerships for spiritual and social development.

SMCC GOAL

Uphold Culture of Excellence in the Areas of Spiritual Formation, Instruction, Research, and Extension, thus producing Graduates that are Globally Competent, Spiritually Embodied, and Socially Responsible.
